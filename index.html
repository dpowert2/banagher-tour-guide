Your Stops

1
Banagher Harbour
Night 0 & 4 Start / End
Marina in town centre — stock up, explore, settle in
🍽️ Eat & Drink
JJ Hough's Singing Pub MUST-DO 🚶 2 min
250-year heritage pub with trad music sessions. Try the local Bo Bristle craft beer.
The Vine House Restaurant 🚶 3 min
Georgian townhouse setting, refined Irish cooking.
Flynn's Bar 🚶 2 min
Proper locals' pub, casual pints.
🏰 History & Heritage
Martello Tower / Fanesker Tower 🚶 5 min
The only inland Martello tower in Ireland. Free to visit.
Fort Eliza 🚶 10 min
Napoleonic-era gun battery overlooking the river.
Seven-Arch Bridge 🚶 2 min
Beautiful stone bridge right in town.
Clonfert Cathedral 🚕 15 min / 10 km
Stunning 12th-century Romanesque doorway — one of Ireland's finest. Free entry.
🌿 Outdoors & Nature
Riverside Walk — Flat and pushchair-friendly along the Shannon.
Open-Air River Swimming Pool — Seasonal, right on the Shannon.
Shannon Callows — Birdlife haven, corncrake habitat.
Pitch & Putt 🚶 5 min
🎯 Activities
Playground at Harbour Kids
Right beside the mooring.
Mid Ireland Adventure — Kayaking, SUP, bike hire.
🛍️ Supplies & Practical
Centra Supermarket 🚶 3 min — Stock up for the trip.
Boat Fuel — Available at the marina. Top up before departure.
🚕 Side Trip
Birr Castle & Demesne MUST-DO Kids 🚕 15 min
120 acres of parkland, the Great Telescope, Science Centre, Ireland's tallest treehouse & adventure playground. Adults ~€12, kids ~€6-7, family ~€34.
2
Shannonbridge
~1h cruise Pass-through (30-60 min)
Quick stop for a famous sunset pub and Napoleonic history
🍽️ Eat & Drink
Luker's Old Bar 🚶 1 min
Famous for sunsets over the bog. Trad music sessions.
🏰 History & Heritage
16-Arch Stone Bridge — Striking span across the Shannon.
Napoleonic Fortification 🚶 5 min
Artillery battery defending the Shannon crossing.
🌿 Outdoors & Nature
Peatland Walks — Explore the midlands bog landscape.
🛍️ Supplies & Practical
Small local shop available. Mooring either side of bridge.
3
Clonmacnoise
~1h from Shannonbridge Save for Day 3 return
One of Europe's great monastic ruins — spectacular approach by water
🍽️ Eat & Drink
Visitor Centre Café — Small café with snacks and hot drinks.
🏰 History & Heritage
Clonmacnoise Monastic Site MUST-DO Kids
Founded 544 AD. Round towers, high crosses, cathedral ruins. Visitor centre has a 20-min history film. Open 10am–5:30pm. Adults ~€8, kids free under 12.
Nuns' Church — Short walk from the main site, beautiful Romanesque arches.
Whiskey Footnote — The Annals of Clonmacnoise (1405) contain the earliest known reference to whiskey in Ireland.
🌿 Outdoors & Nature
Riverside Walk — Along the Shannon from the jetty.
Callows Birdwatching — Seasonal waterfowl on the flood meadows.
🎯 Activities
Explore the Ruins Kids
Ruins are freely explorable with plenty of space for children to run.
4
Athlone
Night 1 & 3 ~1h from Clonmacnoise
Ireland's heartland town — world's oldest pub, castle, great food
🍽️ Eat & Drink
Sean's Bar MUST-DO 🚶 1 min
World's oldest pub (since ~900 AD). Has its own whiskey. Trad music sessions.
Thyme Restaurant 🚶 3 min
Riverside setting, modern Irish cuisine. Book dinner ahead.
The Fatted Calf 🚶 5 min
Charcoal oven cooking, excellent lunch spot.
Kin Khao Thai 🚶 5 min Kids
Family-friendly Thai restaurant.
Jalan Jalan 🚶 5 min
Asian fusion above Peddler Mac's bar.
Docks Off 5th 🚶 5 min
Great date night option.
Left Bank Bistro 🚶 5 min
Lovely for brunch.
🏰 History & Heritage
Athlone Castle MUST-DO Kids 🚶 1 min
12th-century Norman castle with interactive museum. Adults ~€10, family ~€38. Allow 1–1.5 hours.
Luan Gallery 🚶 5 min
Free contemporary art gallery by the river.
St Peter & Paul's Church 🚶 5 min
Beautiful twin-towered church.
🌿 Outdoors & Nature
Shannon Banks Nature Trail Kids
5 km loop, flat and family-friendly. Allow 1.5 hours.
Old Rail Trail Greenway
Flat cycling — 8 km to Moate or 40 km to Mullingar. Bike hire available.
🎯 Activities
Viking Tours Kids
Replica Viking longship from the castle pier. Daily, ~40 min. Viking hats provided!
Athlone Towncentre — Shopping centre for rainy-day browsing.
🛍️ Supplies & Practical
Full town — Tesco, Aldi, pharmacies, everything within walking distance of the quay.
🚕 Side Trip
Kilbeggan Distillery 🚕 20 min
World's oldest licensed distillery (1757). Tours and tastings.
5
Hodson Bay
Night 2 ~45 min from Athlone
Lakeshore luxury — inflatable waterpark, fine dining, floating hot tub
🍽️ Eat & Drink
Hodson Bay Hotel Kids
Waterfront Bar carvery for casual lunch, main restaurant for dinner.
Wineport Lodge 🚕 short cab to Glasson
Lakeside fine dining. Special occasion worthy.
🎯 Activities
Baysports MUST-DO Kids
Ireland's largest inflatable waterpark on Lough Ree. Also kayaking, SUP, pedal boats.
Viking Tours — Also sails from Hodson Bay.
Afterglow Floating Hot Tub
90-min cruise on Lough Ree in a floating hot tub. BYO drinks. Adults only.
Hodson Bay Hotel Pool
Pay-per-use if the weather turns. Good rainy-day backup.
🌿 Outdoors & Nature
Lough Ree Shore Walks — Lovely lakeshore paths.
🚕 Side Trip
Glendeer Pet Farm Kids 🚕 15 min
Animals, fairy trail, obstacle course, indoor and outdoor areas. Great for under-8s.
6
Killinure
Turnaround ~30 min from Hodson Bay
Wild anchorage — pure peace and quiet on Lough Ree
🌿 Outdoors & Nature
Birdwatching — Herons, swans, cormorants, grebes in the sheltered lough.
Fishing — Pike, perch, and bream in calm waters.
Swimming Kids (supervised)
Calm, sheltered water — supervise children at all times.
🛍️ Supplies & Practical
No food or shops — Eat on the boat, or dine in Athlone / Hodson Bay before heading out.
Consider an overnight stay if the weather is settled, otherwise loop back to Athlone.

Practical Notes

🔒 LocksOne lock on the route — Meelick Lock, south of Banagher.
💨 Wind WarningLough Ree gets rough quickly. Stay in the river if winds exceed Force 4.
⛽ FuelAvailable at Banagher marina. Top up before departure.
🛒 GroceriesStock up in Banagher or Athlone — supermarkets near moorings.
🧭 NavigationWell-marked channel markers. Speed limit 5 knots in towns.
📱 SignalGenerally good. Patchy on Lough Ree mid-lake.
